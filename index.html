The Challenge

Today, >80% of our resources are tied to 'Run', limiting our capacity for 'Change' and 'Disrupt'.

The Real Problem: Our Knowledge is Trapped

To build digital colleagues, we first need to structure our vast, unreadable data from PowerPoints, emails, and conversations.

Our Method: Adopt the Developer's Toolkit

We mandate that everyone works with plain text (Markdown), version control (Git), and peer review (Pull Requests). This makes our knowledge structured by default.
